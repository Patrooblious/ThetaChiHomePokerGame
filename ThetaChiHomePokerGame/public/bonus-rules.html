  • Poker Terms
  • Straddles: Voluntary blind bet before deal (usually double big blind); acts last pre-flop. This can also lead into Double Straddles and so on
  • Running it Twice: Deal remaining community cards twice when all-in or at player's discretion; split pot by each board.
  • Chopping Blinds: If only blinds remain, players may agree to retrieve blinds without play.
  • Bomb Pots: All players contribute agreed amount pre-flop; skip pre-flop action; deal flop immediately.
  • Kill Pots: Triggered when a player wins two consecutive pots or hits a threshold; extra blind next hand doubles big blind.
