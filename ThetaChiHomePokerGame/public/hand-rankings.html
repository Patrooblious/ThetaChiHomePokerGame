  • Poker Terms

Royal Flush

A, K, Q, J, 10 of the same suit.

Straight Flush

Five consecutive cards of the same suit.

Four of a Kind

Four cards of the same rank plus any fifth card.

Full House

Three of one rank plus two of another.

Flush

Any five cards of the same suit, not in sequence.

Straight

Five consecutive cards of mixed suits.

Three of a Kind

Three cards of the same rank plus two unrelated cards.

Two Pair

Two different pairs plus one side card.

One Pair

Two cards of the same rank plus three side cards.

High Card

When no other hand is made, the highest card plays.
