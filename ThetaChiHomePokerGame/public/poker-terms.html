  • Poker Terms
  • The Nuts: Best possible hand at any moment.
  • Stacking the Deck: Illegal manipulation of cards.
  • Getting Stacked: Losing all chips in one hand.
  • Tilt: Emotional frustration leading to poor play.
  • Full Tilt: Extreme tilt; step away to cool off.
  • Fish: Weak/inexperienced player.
  • Bad Beat: Losing with a strong hand running into an even stronger one.
  • Dead Money: Chips from folded players in pot.
  • Crying Call: Calling with little chance to win, just to confirm fold.
  • Drawing Dead: No possible outs to win the hand.
  • Freeroll: Chance to win without risk of losing.
  • Runner-Runner: Needing two perfect cards on turn & river to win.
  • Limping In: Calling big blind instead of raising pre-flop (often weak).
  • Muck: To fold without showing cards; pile of folded cards.
  • On the Button: In dealer position; last to act post-flop.
  • Under the Gun (UTG): First to act pre-flop (left of big blind).
  • Short Stack: Chip stack significantly below table average (<50 BB).
  • Heads-Up: Only two players remaining in the hand.
  • Tanking: Taking excessive time to decide during a hand.
  • Call the Clock: Force slow player to act within time limit or forfeit hand.
  • Splashing the Pot: Throwing chips messily into pot.
