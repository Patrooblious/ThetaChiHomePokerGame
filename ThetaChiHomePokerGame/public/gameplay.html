Setup

  • Players: 2–8 players
  • Deck: Standard 52-card deck (no jokers), shuffled each hand
  • Blinds: Small Blind = $0.25; Big Blind = $0.50; rotate clockwise after each hand
  • Chips: Denominations: $0.25, $1, $5; buy-in range: $10–$100
  • Dealer Button: Use a marker to track Dealer; moves one seat clockwise each hand

Gameplay

The Deal

Dealer shuffles and deals two hole cards to each player, starting left of Dealer clockwise. If a card is exposed, misdeal and reshuffle. To speed up, use two decks: small blind shuffles, big blind cuts the other.

Betting Rounds

  1. Pre-Flop: After blinds, each player may Fold, Call, or Raise (min raise = $0.25).
  2. Flop: Burn one card; deal three community cards face-up; betting starts left of Dealer.
  3. Turn: Burn one card; deal fourth community card; another betting round.
  4. River: Burn one card; deal fifth community card; final betting round.

Showdown & Ties

Remaining players reveal hands; last aggressor on River shows first. If no River bet, reveal starts left of Dealer. Higher five-card hand wins; ties split pot & odd chip to left of Dealer. Suit ranking (for non-monetary ties): Spades > Hearts > Diamonds > Clubs.

Betting Structure

  • No-Limit: Players may bet any amount up to their stack.
  • Minimum Raise: Equal to double the previous bet/raise (e.g., bet 2$, min raise $4).
  • All-In & Side Pots: All-ins create side pots; all-in player eligible only for main pot.
