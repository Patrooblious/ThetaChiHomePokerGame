  • Poker Terms
  • No Phones: No phone use during an active hand. Phone use during a hand could result in your turn being skipped or your hand being automatically folded.
  • No Hand if Not Present: Absent players at deal are folded; leaving mid-hand folds automatically.
  • Open Cash Game: Join anytime, cash buy-in only, alternate forms of payment at host's discretion; seats first-come, first-served.
  • Top Up: Buy additional chips between hands up to max buy-in; no buying from other players.
  • Unlimited Rebuys: Rebuy whenever your stack is <1 chip; unlimited rebuys allowed.
  • Table Stakes: Only chips on table can be wagered; no dipping into pockets.
  • One Chip Rule: Single oversized chip without verbal raise = call only; change returned.
  • Trading Chips: Allowed between hands for change-making.
  • Verbal Communication: No obligation to reveal stack size or intentions.
  • Show One, Show All: If a player shows cards to anyone, must show to all.
  • Missed Blinds: Missed blinds paid as "dead money" upon return.
  • In the Dark: Actions declared without seeing next card are binding.
